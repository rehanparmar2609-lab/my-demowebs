Who We Are

Ahmedabad's Favourite Late-Night Spot

Friends Coffee Bar is the go-to haunt for Ahmedabad's college crowd, night owls, and cold-coffee loyalists. Nestled in Goyal Towers, opposite Police Chowky in Panjrapol Sanstha, we've been dishing out the city's most crave-worthy cold coffees, loaded Maggi, and stuffed frankies — long after everywhere else has shut down.

What sets us apart? Our signature hand-run mixture that gives our cold coffee a texture and froth that machines just can't replicate. Whether you want a simple Classic Cold Coffee or a wild Oreo-Schezwan-Peri Peri Maggi at 2 AM — we've got you covered.
