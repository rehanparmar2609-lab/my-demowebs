Who We Are

Ahmedabad's Favourite Late-Night Spot

Friends Coffee Bar is the go-to haunt for Ahmedabad's college crowd, night owls, and cold-coffee loyalists. Nestled in Goyal Towers, opposite Police Chowky in Panjrapol Sanstha — we're your 2 AM plan.

Our signature hand-run mixture gives our cold coffee a texture and froth that machines just can't replicate. Whether you want a Classic Cold Coffee or a wild Triple Threat Maggi at 2 AM — we've got you.
